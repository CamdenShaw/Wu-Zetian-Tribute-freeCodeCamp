Wu Zetian

The first and last female Emperor of China
Wu Zetian seen goading her challengers.

Key points in Wu Zetian's life:

  • 624 CE » Wu Zetian is born Wu Zhao.
  • 638 CE » Wu Zhao becomes concubine of Emperor Taizong.
  • 649 CE » Wu Zhao becomes First Concubine of Emperor Gaozong.
  • 653 CE » Wu Zhao gives birth to sons, angers Gaozong's wife.
  • 654 CE » Wu Zhao gives birth to daughter who is murdered; Gaozong's wife found guilty.
  • 660 CE » Wu Zhao is power behind throne, institues secret police.
  • 666 CE » Wu Zhao leads women to Mount Tai, officiates at religious ceremony.
  • 683 CE » Gaozong dies and Wu Zhao tries to rule through her sons.
  • 690 CE » Wu Zhao proclaims herself Empress Wu Zetian and begins her rule.
  • 697 CE » Wu Zetian's power begins to waver.
  • 704 CE » Wu Zetian is forced to abdicate in favor of her son Zhongzong.
  • 705 CE » The death of Wu Zetian, first and last femal emperor of China.
